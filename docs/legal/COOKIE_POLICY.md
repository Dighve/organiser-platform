# OutMeets Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** December 9, 2025  
**Effective Date:** December 9, 2025

## 1. INTRODUCTION

This Cookie Policy explains how OutMeets ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our Platform.

**What are cookies?** Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

---

## 2. COOKIES WE USE

### 2.1 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are **NECESSARY** for the Platform to function. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `auth_token` | Stores your JWT authentication token | 24 hours | First-party |
| `refresh_token` | Stores your refresh token for session renewal | 7 days | First-party |
| `session_id` | Maintains your session state | Session | First-party |
| `csrf_token` | Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks | Session | First-party |

**Legal Basis:** Strictly necessary for service provision (GDPR Article 6(1)(b))

### 2.2 Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies enhance your experience but are not essential.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|
| `user_preferences` | Remembers your settings (theme, language, etc.) | 1 year | First-party |
| `return_url` | Stores intended destination for seamless login | Session | First-party |
| `search_history` | Remembers recent searches | 30 days | First-party |

**Legal Basis:** Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))

### 2.3 Analytics Cookies (Optional - Not Currently Implemented)

These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Platform.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type | Provider |
|-------------|---------|----------|------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics - Distinguishes users | 2 years | Third-party | Google |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics - Distinguishes users | 24 hours | Third-party | Google |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics - Throttles request rate | 1 minute | Third-party | Google |

**Legal Basis:** Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))

**Opt-out:** You can opt out of Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies (Not Currently Used)

We do NOT currently use marketing or advertising cookies.

If we introduce these in the future, we will:
- Update this policy
- Request your explicit consent
- Provide opt-out options

## 14. TECHNICAL DETAILS

### 14.1 Cookie Attributes

Our cookies use secure attributes:
- **Secure:** Only transmitted over HTTPS
- **HttpOnly:** Not accessible via JavaScript (prevents XSS attacks)
- **SameSite:** Prevents CSRF attacks (Strict or Lax)
- **Domain:** Limited to outmeets.com
- **Path:** Limited to specific paths (e.g., /api/v1)

### 14.2 JWT Token Storage

Our authentication tokens are stored as:
- **Method:** HttpOnly cookies (most secure)
- **Alternative:** Local storage (less secure, but works cross-domain)
- **Current implementation:** Local storage (for development flexibility)
- **Production recommendation:** HttpOnly cookies

## 15. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q1: Can I use OutMeets without cookies?
**A:** No. Essential cookies are required for authentication and security. However, you can disable optional cookies (functional, analytics).

### Q2: Do you sell cookie data to third parties?
**A:** No. We do NOT sell your data to anyone.

### Q3: How long are cookies stored?
**A:** Essential cookies: 24 hours to 7 days. Optional cookies: up to 2 years (Google Analytics). See Section 2 for details.

### Q4: Can I delete cookies?
**A:** Yes. Clear cookies in your browser settings or click "Cookie Settings" in our footer.

### Q5: Do cookies track me across other websites?
**A:** No. Our first-party cookies only work on OutMeets. Third-party cookies (Google OAuth, Cloudinary) may track across sites per their policies.

### Q6: Are cookies safe?
**A:** Yes. Cookies cannot execute code or contain viruses. We use secure cookies with HttpOnly and SameSite attributes.

### Q7: What happens if I block cookies?
**A:** Blocking essential cookies will prevent login and core functionality. Blocking optional cookies will reduce personalization but the site will still work.

## 16. GLOSSARY

- **First-party cookie:** Set by OutMeets directly
- **Third-party cookie:** Set by external services (Google, Cloudinary)
- **Session cookie:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookie:** Stored for a specified duration
- **HttpOnly:** Cookie not accessible via JavaScript
- **Secure:** Cookie only sent over HTTPS
- **SameSite:** Cookie restrictions for cross-site requests
